Overview

Designed a landing page focused on conversion, clarity, and premium visual appeal. Handled mismatch between ice cream brief and chocolate assets by maintaining a unified dessert theme while preserving strong visual consistency.

Problem

The original brief required an ice cream landing page, but the provided visual assets were chocolate-focused. This created inconsistency between product direction, messaging, and visual storytelling. The challenge was to maintain clarity while still delivering a premium dessert experience.

Goals

• Create a visually premium landing page
• Improve product hierarchy and readability
• Design stronger CTA sections for conversion
• Ensure responsive mobile experience
• Maintain cohesive dessert branding throughout

Design Approach

The interface was designed using structured sections, minimal clutter, and strong typography hierarchy. A premium “Single Origin” storytelling section was introduced to enhance product perception and improve emotional engagement with the brand.

Final Desktop Design

The desktop experience focused on premium spacing, strong hero presentation, and conversion-focused product storytelling with modern visual hierarchy.

Mobile Design

The mobile version was designed with responsive layouts, simplified spacing, and clear CTA visibility to maintain usability across smaller screens.

Key Improvements

• Improved content hierarchy for easier scanning
• Strong CTA placement for conversion-focused flow
• Premium product storytelling sections
• Balanced product imagery and information
• Better readability and responsive layout handling

UX Thinking

The experience was designed to guide users naturally from discovery to action through clear visual hierarchy, strategic spacing, and minimal distractions. The overall goal was to create a premium browsing experience that feels elegant while still remaining conversion-focused.

What I Learned

This project helped strengthen my understanding of landing page UX, visual storytelling, responsive layouts, and conversion-focused design thinking. I also explored balancing branding consistency while adapting to asset limitations.
