Milky Mist Landing Page

Designing a premium, conversion-focused dessert experience

View Case Study ↓

Overview

Designed a landing page focused on conversion, clarity, and premium visual appeal. Handled mismatch between ice cream brief and chocolate assets by maintaining a unified dessert theme.

Problem

The brief required an ice cream landing page, but the provided assets were chocolate-based. This created inconsistency in visual direction and messaging.

Goals

• Create a conversion-focused landing page
• Maintain premium visual identity
• Ensure clarity in product sections
• Improve content hierarchy
• Design responsive mobile layout

Design Approach

Used structured sections with clear CTAs and minimal clutter. Introduced a premium "Single Origin" section to highlight product uniqueness and enhance storytelling.

Final Desktop Design

Mobile Design

Key Improvements

• Clear section hierarchy for better navigation
• Strong CTA placement for conversion
• Premium product storytelling (Single Origin)
• Balanced visuals with product information
• Mobile-first responsive layout

UX Thinking

Focused on guiding users from discovery to action through structured sections, visual hierarchy, and minimal distractions.
