StageSeat

A seamless way to discover, choose, and book live event experiences.

View Case Study ↓ Live Prototype

Overview

StageSeat is a desktop-based platform designed to simplify event discovery and ticket booking with a smooth, guided experience.

Problem

Users face cluttered layouts, confusing seat selection, and poor decision support while booking tickets.

Solution

A structured booking flow with clear hierarchy, simplified seat selection, and AI-assisted recommendations.

Key Features

• Smart search & filters
• Interactive seat selection
• AI seat recommendations
• Simplified checkout
• Booking tracking

Design Screens

Design Thinking

Focused on reducing cognitive load using structured layouts, progressive disclosure, and clear navigation paths.

Outcome

A clean, intuitive booking experience with faster decision-making and reduced complexity.
