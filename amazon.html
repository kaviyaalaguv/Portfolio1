Amazon Redesign

Simplifying shopping by reducing clutter and improving product discovery.

View Case Study ↓ Live Prototype

Overview

Redesign of Amazon mobile home screen to reduce clutter, improve hierarchy, and enhance product discovery.

Problem

The original design had excessive banners and repeated sections, making it difficult for users to find relevant products without scrolling extensively.

Goals

• Reduce visual clutter
• Improve hierarchy
• Reduce scrolling
• Improve product discovery
• Highlight deals clearly

Final Design

Key Improvements

• Replaced large banners with compact sections
• Introduced structured card layout
• Horizontal scrolling for deals
• Clear separation of content
• Better spacing and alignment

UX Thinking

Maintained three recommendation sections targeting different user intent: browsing, considering, and purchasing — improving engagement and conversions.

Design System

• 8px spacing system
• Card-based layout
• Rounded corners
• Minimal color palette
• Consistent typography
