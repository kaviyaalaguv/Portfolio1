Overview

PrepTrack is a personalized preparation planner designed to help competitive exam aspirants structure their study routines, maintain consistency, and track daily progress through guided planning workflows.

Problem

Students preparing for competitive exams often struggle with maintaining consistency, creating realistic schedules, and tracking long-term progress effectively.

Solution

A smart planner that generates structured daily schedules based on user goals, preparation level, and study timelines while reducing planning fatigue.

Design Screens

Key UX Decisions

• Personalized study planning flow
• Simple progress tracking system
• Reduced cognitive overload using structured layouts
• Clear visual hierarchy for daily schedules
• Focused mobile-first interaction patterns

What I Learned

This project helped strengthen my understanding of educational product design, habit-based user behavior, structured planning systems, and designing interfaces that support long-term engagement.
