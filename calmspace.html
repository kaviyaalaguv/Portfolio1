CalmSpace

A mindful reset app that helps users pause, reset, and refocus.

View Case Study ↓ Live Prototype

Overview

CalmSpace helps users handle overwhelming emotions through simple, guided steps that reduce mental load and encourage action.

Problem

Users feel overwhelmed, anxious, or low energy, but existing apps are complex and require effort instead of helping instantly.

Solution

A minimal experience that allows users to select their mood and follow a guided, step-by-step reset.

Entry Experience

A calm entry screen invites users to begin without overwhelming choices.

Mood Selection

Users select how they feel to receive a personalized guided flow.

Guided Experience

Steps are broken into simple actions, reducing cognitive load and encouraging completion.

Focus & Completion

Soft progress indicators guide users without pressure and reinforce small wins.

Key Decisions

• Step-by-step guidance instead of instructions
• No strict timers to avoid stress
• Minimal UI for calm interaction
• Emotion-based flow instead of generic actions
